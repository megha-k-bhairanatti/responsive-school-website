Welcome To Little Champ

The Preschool program at Little Champ is designed for children between the ages of 3 to 6. We aim to provide our students with the opportunity to develop self-awareness, teamwork skills, and grow to be lifelong learners and caring citizens.

Language Lesson

Children can learn to speak, read and write 200 words through a series of lessons and games.

Meals Provided

Food provided to children in schools is consistent with current dietary recommendations.

Professional Teachers

The school has teachers who represent the best in the profession and set the highest standard for good practice.

Smart Education

Comprising of intelligent education systems, school aims to increase the capabilities of next-gen students.

Transportation

The school provides transport facility with own buses for bringing in students from different localities.

Toys & Games

Include board games, books, art supplies, construction sets, puzzles and outdoor toys.
